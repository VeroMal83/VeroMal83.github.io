Technical Advisory Consultation

This consultation is a paid, time-boxed advisory session intended to determine whether a formal professional engagement is appropriate.

It is designed for individuals or organizations seeking clarity before commissioning work. It is not intended for free troubleshooting, system review, or informal advice.

What This Consultation Is

  • High-level technical discussion
  • Scoping and qualification for potential engagements
  • Professional advisory exchange

What This Consultation Is Not

  • System audits or security assessments
  • Configuration review or troubleshooting
  • Architecture design or implementation guidance
  • A substitute for contracted professional services

No operational or implementation work is performed during consultations.

Format

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Medium: Voice or video
  • No screen sharing
  • No recordings
  • No advance materials reviewed

Deliverables

None. Following the session, you may be informed whether a formal engagement is recommended and what form it would take.

Fee

  • Flat, prepaid consultation fee
  • Non-refundable
  • May be credited toward a formal engagement at my discretion

Important Notice

Requests seeking actionable guidance, implementation advice, or system evaluation are fulfilled only under a signed agreement and Statement of Work.
