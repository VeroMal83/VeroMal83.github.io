Technical Advisory Consultation

This is a paid, time-limited advisory session intended to determine whether a formal professional engagement is appropriate.

It is designed for principals or representatives seeking clarity on scope and suitability before commissioning work. No operational work, system evaluation, or configuration review is performed.

Purpose

  • High-level technical discussion
  • Scoping and qualification for potential engagements
  • Professional advisory exchange

Scope Boundaries

  • System audits or security assessments are not included
  • Configuration review or troubleshooting is not provided
  • Architecture design or implementation guidance is not performed
  • This session is not a substitute for a contracted engagement

Format

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Medium: Voice or video
  • No screen sharing
  • No recordings
  • No advance materials reviewed

Outcomes

No deliverables are provided. Following the session, you may be advised whether a formal engagement is appropriate and what form it could take.

Fee

  • Flat, prepaid consultation fee
  • Non-refundable
  • May be credited toward a formal engagement, at discretion
